Pizza

IngredientAmountPicture
Pizza Dough1 ball (300 g)
Tomato Sauce½ cup
Mozzarella1 cup, shredded
Directions:
1. Pre‑heat oven to 475 °F (245 °C).
2. Stretch dough on a floured peel.
3. Spread sauce and add cheese.
4. Bake 12‑14 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
5. Let rest 2 min.
